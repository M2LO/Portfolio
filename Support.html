ARTIST SUPPORT

TEAMMBL

Me & my DJ partner Lauren Z. Ray launched TEAMMBL.com in 2016 which is now one of the biggest music producer communities & websites in the world. In 2018, we sold our home in Los Angeles & everything we owned to tour the world full-time, DJ shows, give speeches, and collaborate with other artists & brands.

Dada Life are a Swedish DJ duo formed in 2006, which consists of Olle Cornéer and Stefan Engblom. They have released three albums, a number of singles and remixes and one compilation. The duo had early chart success in Belgium and the Netherlands, while their second album The Rules of Dada reached the Billboard 200. They are known for their humour during their live performances, while their name is a homage to the absurdist movement of the early twentieth century. They have released two audio plugins programmed by Tailored Noise: Endless Smile, a drop editor plugin and Sausage Fattener, a saturator.

FHM

Launched in 2014, what began as a community of house music lovers has developed into a record label with a clear focus putting artists first and providing the finest releases to an ever-growing fanbase. From our home in Rotterdam, The Netherlands to global live events, Future House Music continues to make an impact on an international scale by sharing our music and exciting our fans around the world.

Wildcrow, is a producer from Spain that started making music as a hobby back in 2013. The most produced genre of this artist is Progressive House but he also likes to try other genres like House, Euphoric Hardstyle and Future bass/trap amongst others. His music has been played by Hardwell, Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, Nicky Romero, Mike Williams, Bingo Players and Dash Berlin to name a few. He went from releasing music for free to an official release on Revealed Recordings (Hardwell's label).

SEVERMAN

Back in 2014 I discovered my passion for Electronic Dance Music through the former number 1 DJ Hardwell and the rise of Martin Garrix. After six years of practicing my skills, in 2020 I gained some traction from tutorials I began uploading on my YouTube channel. I also got my songs signed on record labels such as Hardwell's Revealed Radar, AirwaveMusicTV & more.

Pablo Lincango, known as J4CKO, is an electronic music producer and composer. Born in Quito, Ecuador, he began his career as a musician in 2018 at the young age of 15 after attending his first concert, it was at that moment he decided become a DJ and takes the initiative to publish his own works, which receive good criticism from his audience.

LOSTVOLTS

LostVolts is an Indian Music Producer whose emotional, melodic & energetic has made quite an impact on the new-age revival of Progressive House. He fell in love with Electronic Music when he was 15, and has been constantly improving his sounds and skills and is heavily inspired by none other than HARDWELL. He started his career by winning the first ever REVEALED RECORDINGS x IZOTOPE Producer's Contest and has released various tracks on Revealed Recordings, Smash The House, Glow Records, FeelQ Recordings etc.

Stefan Perevski (Arvenius) is a 13 year old producer from Skopje, Macedonia. He has been producing for around 2 years, and his main genre is Progressive House. Back in October 2019, he found his passion for EDM (Especially Prog. House). The producers/DJ's that inspired him to start producing were Manse, Jim Yosef, Tobu and CHRSTN.
